QA Engineer (GameFi)

Time to fill: 20 days

Company: The company develops games and software for game developers based on blockchain technology

Recruitment challenges

• Candidates were required to complete an extensive test task;

• The company provided delayed feedback on the results of the test task;

• Three recruitment agencies, including us, were working on the vacancy.

Key requirements

• Critical to have experience as a QA Engineer in blockchain-crypto gaming projects for at least 3 years (Web or Mobile);

• Proficiency in test design techniques;

• English language proficiency at C1 level.

Who we found

• A QA Engineer from a competing company with 5 years of experience in blockchain-crypto gaming projects.

How we achieved this

• Compiled a list of donor companies and contacted all QA engineers working for them.

• At the stage of the first SMS, we sent candidates the test task so they could familiarize themselves with it and decide whether they were ready to take it.

Result

• Introduced 6 QA engineers who fully met the requirements, 2 of whom successfully completed the test task;

• Both candidates successfully passed the technical interview, but only one accepted the offer;

• We successfully continue our collaboration with the company for other positions.
