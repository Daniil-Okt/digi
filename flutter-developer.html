Flutter Developer (Blockchain)

Deadline: 7 days

Company: Manufactures heaters that generate income by mining bitcoins.

Recruitment challenges

• The role was to work in a two-person team without a CTO, QA, DevOps, or technical writers.

• Excellent knowledge of English required.

Key requirements

• At least 3 years of experience with Flutter.

• Experience with GitHub or similar platforms (Git or SVN).

• Experience in developing fintech products/IoT.

• Successful experience working in small teams (without CTO, QA, and technical writers).

Who we found

• A Flutter developer was found in 3 working days. A total of 7 resumes were sent. The finalist candidate was the second one we referred for the position. When compared with other candidates, he was the best. The candidate accepted the offer the same day.

How we achieved this

• The search was conducted by a recruiter who had previously filled Flutter developer positions in other companies. A sourcer was also assigned to allow the recruiter to conduct up to 6 interviews per day.

• The vacancy had a high search priority as it was crucial to fill it quickly.

Result

• The candidate started work the next day after the offer, on a 20-hour per week basis, and fully commenced full-time duties two weeks later.

• The client was satisfied and decided to continue searching for another specialist for the team, as the timeframe allowed.

• We successfully continue our collaboration with the company for other positions.
